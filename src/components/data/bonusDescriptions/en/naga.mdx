## What is NAGA and how the promotion works

**NAGA** is a fintech investment platform operated by **Naga Markets Europe Ltd**, an investment firm authorized and regulated by CySEC. The platform allows trading on a wide range of assets, specifically offering direct market access via a **Real Shares** account.

With the **"Refer a Friend"** campaign, currently active until the available stock of rewards is exhausted, NAGA rewards new users and those who invite them with **Free Shares** (free shares or fractions of them). The value of each share is determined by the market price at the time of credit and can range from a minimum of **$25** up to over **$100**.

Official requirements link: [https://cms.naga.com/Naga_Markets_Europe_Ltd_Refer_a_Friend_V1_2430ec881d.pdf](https://cms.naga.com/Naga_Markets_Europe_Ltd_Refer_a_Friend_V1_2430ec881d.pdf)

### Main Requirements

To properly participate in the campaign and receive the reward, you must meet the following criteria:

* **Specific Account:** The promotion is reserved exclusively for clients holding a **Real Shares** account. CFD accounts are explicitly excluded from the campaign.
* **Minimum Deposit:** A deposit of at least **100 USD** (or equivalent in another currency) made directly into the Real Shares account is required. 
    * *Note: The deposited amount is withdrawable at any time. However, closing the account or withdrawing all deposited funds immediately after the Free Share has been allocated can lead to disqualification and reclaiming of the reward.*
* **Documents:** Registration, completion of the onboarding process, and identity verification in accordance with the company's requirements via a friend's referral link. Proof of residence is also required (a bank statement is sufficient).
* **Referral Limits:** You can receive rewards for a maximum of **10 friends** successfully referred.
* **Lock-Up Period:** Once the Free Share is received, its monetary value (or the funds acquired from its sale) is subject to a **30-day** withdrawal suspension period.

### Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to obtain your free share:

1.  **Sign-up:** Click on your friend's **referral link** to start the registration.
2.  **Verification:** Complete the onboarding and get verified by the company by uploading the required documents. Proof of residence is also requested, so a bank statement is needed (it is better to avoid Revolut's statement, as it might not be accepted).
3.  **Participation:** Activate your Real Shares account and officially register for the Free Shares Campaign.
4.  **Deposit:** Make a deposit of at least **100 USD** into your Real Shares account.
5.  **Bonus Reception:** Within **3 Business Days** from completing all the steps, both you and the person who referred you will receive the Free Share directly in your Real Shares account.
6.  **Maintenance:** Avoid violating the terms, manipulating the promo, or emptying/closing the account right after the credit to prevent the reward from being reclaimed or the account suspended.
