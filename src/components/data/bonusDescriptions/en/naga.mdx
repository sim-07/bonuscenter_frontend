## What is NAGA and How the Promotion Works

**NAGA** is a fintech investment platform operated by **Naga Markets Europe Ltd**, an investment firm authorized and regulated by CySEC. The platform allows users to trade a wide range of assets, specifically providing direct market access through a **Real Shares** account.

With the **"Refer a Friend"** campaign, officially extended throughout **June 2026**, NAGA rewards new users and their referrers with **Free Shares** (fractional or full real shares). The value of each share is determined by the market price at the time of credit and can range from a minimum of **$25** to over **$100**.

---

### Main Requirements

To successfully participate in the campaign and claim your reward, you must meet the following criteria:

* **Specific Account:** The promotion is exclusively reserved for clients holding a **Real Shares (DMA)** account. CFD accounts are explicitly excluded from this campaign.
* **Minimum Deposit (Referee):** The referred friend must complete a minimum deposit of at least **100 USD** (or currency equivalent) directly into their Real Shares account.
* **Balance Requirement (Referrer):** In order to generate valid bonuses, the Referrer (the person inviting) must also hold and continuously maintain a net balance of at least **100 USD** in their Real Shares account. If the balance falls below this threshold due to a withdrawal, any pending rewards are forfeited, and the ability to send further referrals is permanently lost.
* **Geographical Restrictions:** Only residents of selected countries under the CySEC license are eligible. Residents of **Spain**, Belgium, France, and the Czech Republic are **explicitly excluded** from the promotion.
* **Documentation:** Successful registration and completion of the onboarding and identity verification process via a friend's unique referral link. Proof of residence (e.g., a bank statement) is also required.
* **Referral Limits:** You can receive rewards for a maximum of **10 successfully referred friends** (totaling up to 10 Free Shares overall).
* **Lock-Up Period (45 days) & Reset Effect:** Once the Free Share is credited, its monetary value (or any funds derived from selling it) is subject to a **45-day withdrawal restriction**.
    * *Crucial Note: If you receive multiple Free Shares at different times, each new reward delivery resets the 45-day timer, restarting it from scratch for all accumulated rewards currently in the account.*

### Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to claim your free share:

1.  **Sign Up:** Click your friend's **referral link** to initiate the registration process.
2.  **Verification:** Upload the required identity documents (ID card or driver's license) and a valid proof of address, such as a traditional bank statement (it is recommended to avoid Revolut statements to prevent potential automated rejections by the compliance system).
3.  **Deposit:** Fund your **Real Shares (DMA)** account with at least **100 USD** (or currency equivalent).
4.  **Hold Period:** Leave your deposited funds in the account for approximately **3 business days** until the bonus is fully processed. Making an early withdrawal that drops the account balance below $100 will trigger an immediate reward forfeiture for both parties.
5.  **Claim Reward:** Within 72 hours of completing all previous steps, both you and your referrer will receive a Free Share directly into your respective Real Shares accounts. You can sell the share or withdraw its value once the lock-up period expires.
