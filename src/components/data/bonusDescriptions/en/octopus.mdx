### The guide to unlocking the bonus

If you are the friend being invited, make sure you have everything you need on hand before clicking the link. To activate the contract with Octopus Energy, you will need:

* **An old bill:** You will need this to retrieve the **POD** code (for electricity) or **PDR** code (for gas).
* **Identity document:** Valid.
* **IBAN code:** For automatic bill payment (Direct Debit).

It is crucial that the friend has not been an Octopus customer in the past and completes the procedure exclusively via the received link; otherwise, the system will not be able to associate the invite with your account.

The final and most important step is **activation**. The bonus does not appear immediately after registration is finished, but at the moment the supply becomes technically active (i.e., when Octopus officially becomes the new supplier, usually the first day of the month following acceptance).
