## What is Finst and how the bonus works

**FINST** is a cryptocurrency exchange wallet app based in the Netherlands, authorized by the Dutch Authority for the Financial Markets (AFM) under the MiCA regulation, which allows you to invest in crypto with ultra-low fees.

Promotion details:
* Receive a **random bonus between €10 and €200 in Bitcoin** if you register through a referral link and complete the trading requirements.
* Earn **between €10 and €200 for each friend** you bring to Finst, provided they meet the required trading volume thresholds.
* The bonus is credited in the following month (e.g., early July) in the form of Bitcoin directly into your wallet.

---

### Main requirements

To successfully participate in the promotion and receive the reward, make sure to meet these criteria by **30/06/2026**:

* **Minimum Deposit:** There is no fixed minimum deposit required, but you must add enough funds to complete the required trading volume.
* **Trading Volume:** The invited friend must execute buy or sell transactions for a total of at least **€1,000**.
  * *Example:* By depositing €200, 5 trades (e.g., 3 buys and 2 sells) are enough to reach the required volume of €1,000.
* **Identity Verification:** You must complete the identity verification process (KYC) with a valid ID document and biometric recognition.
* **Rewards Limit:** You can receive a maximum of **3 bonuses per promotional period** through your referrals.

### Step-by-step guide

Follow these steps to unlock the bonus:

1. **Registration:** Click on the personal referral link found on this page and create your account.
2. **KYC:** Complete the account opening by uploading photos of your ID document and following the guided verification procedure.
3. **Deposit:** Make a bank transfer (using your personal IBAN provided by the app) or deposit funds to start trading.
4. **Trading:** Use the funds to trade cryptocurrencies (e.g., EUR/BTC or stablecoins) until you generate a total volume of at least **€1,000** before the deadline.
5. **Bonus Receipt:** If you have completed all conditions by 30/06, you and your referrer will receive the **Bitcoin bonus during the month of July**.

💡 **Tip:** Finst applies a flat and transparent 0.15% fee with no hidden spreads. To precisely monitor your generated trading volume and related fees, you can check the summary inside the "Transactions" section of the app at any time.
