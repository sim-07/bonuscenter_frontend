## What is ho. Mobile and how the bonus works

**ho. Mobile** is a Vodafone virtual operator (MVNO) offering simple, transparent plans with no hidden costs. Utilizing the Vodafone network, it guarantees excellent coverage and speeds in 4G or 5G at competitive prices.

The **"ho. Tanti amici"** program (also known as "Porta tutti in ho.") allows both the inviter and the invitee to receive a financial benefit in the form of a free phone recharge.

The bonus is issued as **bonus credit** that can be used to pay for the monthly renewals of your plan, effectively making the service free for the months following the reward attainment.

---

### Main Requirements

To participate correctly and receive your free recharge, ensure you meet the following criteria:

* **Invite Link:** The activation of the new SIM must necessarily take place via the personal link provided by a friend.
* **Activation:** You must complete the purchase and activation of the SIM (via app, newsstand, or home delivery).
* **First Renewal:** The €5 bonus is not issued immediately, but after the new customer completes their **first monthly renewal**.
* **SIM Status:** Both SIMs (the inviter's and the invitee's) must be active at the time the bonus is issued.
* The reward consists of a free recharge credited directly to the SIM's remaining balance.

---

### Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to ensure you don't miss the bonus:

#### If you received an invitation:
1. Open the **link** sent to you by your friend.
2. Choose your preferred **offer** from those available on the landing page.
3. Proceed with the purchase and **activate your ho. SIM** (via video-identification or at a retail point, depending on your choice).
4. Use the SIM normally and wait for the **first renewal** of the offer (the second month).
5. You will receive a **€5 free recharge** directly on your remaining credit immediately after the renewal.

#### If you want to invite:
1. Access the official ho. app and open the **menu**.
2. Enter the dedicated **"Porta tutti in ho."** section.
3. Select **"ho. Tanti amici"** at the top.
4. Click on **"Invite"** and share your personal link with whomever you like to activate a new SIM.
5. Wait for your friend to activate the SIM and complete their first renewal to receive your **€5 free recharge**.

Once the bonus credit has been accumulated, you can view it directly in the "My Recharges" area or on the main dashboard of the ho. Mobile app.
